Practice

The thread underneath the work.

I rarely start with slogans or campaigns. My instinct is diagnostic — before expression, I try to understand the structural conditions that produce misunderstanding in the first place. Whether the subject is a governance platform, a restaurant, or a cultural magazine, the discipline is the same: reduce complexity without reducing sophistication.

Diagnosis before prescription

Symptoms point to deeper architecture beneath the sentence-level surface.

Structural coherence

Every project is approached as a coherent system — language, perception, and institution reinforcing one another. Two research frameworks, RAFU and Asukshira, sit underneath this work as methodology.

Independent ventures

LandKnot, Waysoroh, and KANINIFI are my own — proof of how I think when no client is asking.

About

Mahdi Singaparado is an author, brand strategist, and cultural researcher based in Bima, Indonesia.

His work moves between two registers that turn out to be the same discipline. Writing Alraya's menu meant sitting with real details first — Sampungu's fishermen, the aquaculture farm in Waworada Bay, the koi pond guests actually sit beside — before a single line got written. LandKnot, Waysoroh, and KANINIFI came from asking the same question in different material: what does this actually do, and where has the explanation stopped matching it.

The full documentation of that work lives in the Vault. The ongoing writing lives in the Archives.
